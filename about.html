About DATA Mobility Partners

For over two decades, we've worked at the intersection of transportation planning and human services — because mobility is both a systems problem and a deeply personal one.

Our Mission

Connecting People to Possibility

DATA Mobility Partners is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to improving transportation access across Northern Virginia. We believe that mobility is a fundamental right — not a privilege — and that everyone deserves the ability to get from here to there with dignity and reliability.

We operate on two fronts: professional transportation demand management services that make communities more efficient, and direct human services that get seniors and veterans where they need to go.

Our Vision

A Region Where No One Is Left Behind

We envision a Northern Virginia where transportation barriers no longer prevent people from accessing healthcare, employment, education, or community life. Where seniors can age in place with dignity. Where veterans receive the care they've earned. Where every community member has the mobility they need to thrive.

Until that vision is fully realized, we will keep driving — literally and figuratively — toward a more connected, more equitable, more mobile community.

Our Story

A History of Service

2001

DATA Is Founded

The Dulles Area Transportation Association is established as a Transportation Management Association serving employers and commuters in the Dulles corridor of Northern Virginia.

2005

Expanding the Mission

Recognizing the need for human services transportation, DATA begins piloting programs for seniors and individuals with disabilities in Fairfax County.

2010

Venture Out Launches

The Venture Out program officially launches, offering group travel experiences for seniors and veterans — from farmers markets to museum visits and social gatherings.

2015

Enhanced Mobility Designation

DATA is designated as a regional coordinator for FTA Section 5310 Enhanced Mobility programs, expanding the scope and reach of services to underserved populations.

2018

Cook for Vets Begins

The Cook for Vets program launches, bringing home-cooked meals and human connection to homebound veterans across Northern Virginia.

2023

Rebranding as DATA Mobility Partners

The organization rebrands as DATA Mobility Partners, reflecting the evolution from a TMA-only organization to a comprehensive mobility nonprofit serving both professional and human services needs.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Dignity

Every person we serve — regardless of age, ability, or income — deserves to be treated with respect and compassion. Our services are designed to preserve independence, not diminish it.

Community

Transportation is about connection. We build bridges — between people and places, between organizations and residents, between the need for mobility and the means to provide it.

Impact

We measure success not in miles driven, but in lives changed. Every ride is an opportunity to restore access to healthcare, nutrition, social connection, and the simple freedom of movement.
