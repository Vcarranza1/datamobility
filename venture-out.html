Venture Out

Group travel experiences that take seniors and veterans beyond their front doors — to farmers markets, museums, restaurants, and back into the world they love.

About the Program

More Than a Trip — It's a Lifeline

For many older adults and veterans, leaving the house becomes increasingly difficult. Physical limitations, loss of driving privileges, or simply the absence of someone to go with can turn the world outside into an unreachable place.

Venture Out changes that. We organize group outings — carefully planned, fully supported, and designed for joy. Participants travel together by bus or van to destinations across Northern Virginia and the D.C. metro area, with trained staff and volunteers ensuring comfort and safety every step of the way.

Where We Go

Popular Venture Out Destinations

From the simple pleasure of a farmers market to the wonder of a museum visit, every outing is an opportunity to reconnect.

Farmers Markets

Weekly trips to local farmers markets where participants can buy fresh produce, enjoy live music, and socialize with neighbors and vendors.

Museums & Cultural Sites

Guided visits to Smithsonian museums, historical sites, and cultural institutions in the D.C. metro area — experiences many thought were no longer possible.

Restaurant Outings

Monthly lunch outings to local restaurants, giving participants the simple but meaningful pleasure of a meal out with friends in a social setting.

Scenic Day Trips

Seasonal trips to parks, botanical gardens, holiday light displays, and scenic destinations throughout Virginia and Maryland.

Community Events

County fairs, holiday celebrations, health expos, and senior-focused events that keep participants engaged with their communities.

Shopping Trips

Organized trips to malls and shopping centers, giving participants the independence to browse, purchase gifts, and enjoy a day out.

Eligibility

Who Can Participate?

Venture Out is open to seniors (60+) and veterans of any age living in Fairfax County, Loudoun County, and Prince William County. There is no cost to participate — all trips are fully funded through grants and donations.

  • Seniors aged 60 and older
  • Veterans of all ages and service branches
  • Residents of Fairfax, Loudoun, and Prince William Counties
  • Individuals with mobility limitations welcome (wheelchair-accessible vehicles available)
  • No income requirements — the program is free

Special Initiative

Cook for Vets

Born from the Venture Out program, Cook for Vets extends our reach to homebound veterans who can no longer travel. Community volunteers prepare home-cooked meals and deliver them with a warm smile and a moment of connection.

It's more than food — it's a reminder that their service is remembered and their well-being matters. Every meal delivered says: "Thank you for your service. You are not forgotten."
