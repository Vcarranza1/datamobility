Serving Northern Virginia Since 2001

Mobility for All

In Northern Virginia, thousands of seniors, veterans, and individuals with disabilities face a barrier most people never think about: getting from here to there. DATA Mobility Partners exists to change that.

What We Do

Two Sides of the Same Mission

We work at the intersection of professional transportation planning and direct human services — because mobility is both a systems problem and a deeply personal one.

Professional Services

TMA Hub

We partner with developers, employers, and planners to build smarter transportation systems across Northern Virginia. From transportation demand management to commuter programs, we connect people to efficient travel options.

Human Services

Community Hub

We serve seniors and veterans directly — getting them to farmers markets, medical appointments, and back into the world. Every ride is a lifeline, and every trip restores independence and dignity.

Explore Community Programs →
Featured Program

Cook for Vets

Our Cook for Vets program delivers home-cooked meals prepared by community volunteers directly to the homes of veterans who can no longer easily leave their homes. It's more than food — it's connection, it's gratitude, and it's a reminder that their service hasn't been forgotten.

Why It Matters

A Ride Is Never Just a Ride

For a veteran who served this country, a trip to the VA clinic isn't optional. For a senior living alone, the weekly run to the grocery store is the difference between eating well and going without. For someone with a disability, a reliable ride to work means independence — not dependence.

We don't just move people. We restore access to life.
