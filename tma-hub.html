Professional Services & TMA Hub

We partner with developers, employers, and planners to build smarter, more sustainable transportation systems across Northern Virginia and the greater Washington metropolitan region.

What We Do

What Is a TMA?

A Transportation Management Association (TMA) is a nonprofit, member-driven organization that applies transportation demand management (TDM) strategies to reduce congestion, improve mobility, and create more livable communities.

DATA serves as the designated TMA for Fairfax County and partners with jurisdictions across Northern Virginia to deliver commuter programs, employer outreach, and development-related transportation solutions.

Our Services

TDM & Commuter Solutions

We deliver measurable results for employers, developers, and jurisdictions seeking to reduce single-occupancy vehicle trips and improve employee commutes.

Employer Outreach

We work directly with employers to implement commuter benefits, transit subsidies, telework policies, and rideshare programs that reduce congestion and improve employee satisfaction.

Vanpool & Rideshare

We organize and support vanpool programs connecting commuters across Northern Virginia, reducing vehicle miles traveled and providing affordable commute alternatives.

Developer TDM Plans

We create comprehensive transportation demand management plans required for new developments, ensuring projects meet county and state TDM requirements while maximizing mobility options.

Transit Planning

We provide technical assistance to jurisdictions on transit route planning, service optimization, and first/last mile connectivity solutions.

Data & Analytics

We leverage commuter surveys, trip-reduction data, and regional analytics to measure program effectiveness and demonstrate ROI for stakeholders.

Regional Coordination

We coordinate with VDOT, DRPT, NVTC, and other regional partners to align local TDM programs with broader transportation goals and funding opportunities.
